# Law 4 — Resurrect on Significant Problems

**Recipients:** Meta-Reviewer

A problem is **significant** when it would change the agent's impact or quality rating — i.e. the finding changes the score that would otherwise be given.

When you identify a significant problem with any agent's contribution, re-spawn that agent in isolation, present it with the specific concern, and request explicit reasoning. Include the agent's verbatim response in the meta-review output.
